Frequently Asked Questions

FOR Individual

We offer professional mental health support through therapy, counselling, and wellbeing services. Our work is grounded in evidence based practices and delivered by trained, ethical mental health professionals.

All our practitioners are qualified, trained, and vetted mental health professionals. We prioritise ethical practice, continuous learning, and supervision to ensure high standards of care.

You do not need to be in crisis to seek therapy. If you are feeling overwhelmed, stuck, emotionally exhausted, or simply curious about understanding yourself better, therapy can help. You are welcome exactly where you are.

Your first session is a space to share what brings you here at your own pace. There is no pressure to have everything figured out. The therapist will focus on understanding you, your context, and what support might be most helpful.

Yes. Confidentiality is central to our work. All sessions and personal information are handled with care and in line with ethical and legal guidelines. Any limits to confidentiality will always be explained clearly.

We currently offer both online and offline (Bangalore) sessions to make care more accessible. Details about formats, availability, and future offerings will be shared on our website.

We help match you with a therapist based on your needs, preferences, and goals. If at any point the fit does not feel right, we encourage open conversation and will support you in finding a better match.

That is completely okay. Many people start therapy feeling unsure or nervous. Our role is to make the process feel safe, respectful, and non-intimidating.

Technology may support certain non-clinical aspects of our work, but therapy itself is always human-led. We do not use AI to replace therapists or make clinical decisions.

You can begin by reaching out through our website or on whatsapp. From there, we will guide you through the next steps and help you find the support that feels right for you.
